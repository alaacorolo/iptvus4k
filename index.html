FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

IPTV stands for Internet Protocol Television. It delivers television programming and video content through your high-speed internet connection instead of traditional cable or satellite formats. This allows you to stream channels and movies on-demand from any compatible device.

Our activation process is fast and efficient. After verifying your purchase on WhatsApp, our support agents usually set up your account and send details within 5 to 15 minutes.

Our service works on almost any device: Amazon Firestick/Fire TV, Smart TVs (LG, Samsung, Sony), Android Box, Apple TV, iOS (iPhone/iPad), Android devices, Windows/Mac PC, MAG, and Formuler. We provide step-by-step installation guides for each device.

Our standard subscription allows 1 connection at a time. If you want to watch on multiple devices concurrently, you can purchase an additional connection line by contacting us on WhatsApp.

We recommend a stable internet speed of at least 15 Mbps for smooth streaming in High Definition (HD) and 30+ Mbps for Ultra HD / 4K content to prevent any buffering.
